Why Latch?

Contract negotiation is the most time-consuming, costly, and difficult component of the contract lifecycle, and is barely any easier now than when lawyers were using fax machines.

Large language models like GPT-4 have unlocked the ability to solve many contract negotiation problems at scale. No company has figured out what the right UX for this looks like yet, but we believe that we’re getting close.

Latch has raised a pre-seed round from leading investors including GD1, Daniel Gross, Craft Ventures, and Austen Allred.

What would you be doing?

We are looking for motivated and curious Software Engineers who can help us make our users’ lives better.

You will have a unique opportunity to be a founding member of this team, helping shape how we impact customers at scale.

Our tech stack includes:

- Typescript / React / Next.js / Node.js
- OpenAI / LangChain
- Firebase

- Chakra UI

- Experience working in a startup environment is preferred but not required.
- Are excited about the adventure of building a company!

Ideally, you have at least 1-2 years of experience as a software engineer.

Description: Apple’s SoC (system-on-chip) Silicon Validation Team is looking for a Full Stack Developer to develop, enhance, and maintain internal infrastructure and tooling geared towards the validation and debug of Apple silicon. You will utilize your software development skills to ensure silicon engineers have the best tools to validate our silicon.

Key Qualifications:

- Server application development in Python 3

- API development (REST, GraphQL, gRPC)
- Database usage and development (Postgres, MySQL, MongoDB)
- Good understanding of at least one web framework (aiohttp, Flask, Starlette, etc.)
- Test driven development
- Advanced Unix shell user

You will also develop web UIs and dashboards for managing test devices, viewing test results, visualizing internal chip state critical for debugging hardware failures. Also, you will be involved in developing solutions to support and enhance infrastructure in our test labs.

Education & Experience:

BS and a minimum of 3 years relevant industry experience

Additional Requirements:

- Data structures and algorithms
- Microservices architecture

- Python AsyncIO development
